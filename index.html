The Listening Lamp

Illuminating Short Stories, One Episode at a Time

The Listening Lamp is an enchanting podcast that illuminates the human experience through immersive and thought-provoking short stories. Short stories that illuminate the soul, each episode explores the profound connections between the spiritual and the scientific, the magical and the mundane, offering listeners a reflective journey.

Our stories range from moral dilemmas to transformative journeys, uncovering the ripple effects of every choice we make. Join us to discover the extraordinary hidden within the most ordinary moments.
